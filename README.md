# 📊 U.S. Retail & Food Sales Analysis

Analysis of top-performing retail and food sales across various industries in the United States, using data from the U.S. Census Bureau Monthly Retail Trade Survey.

---

## 📁 Dataset

- **Source:** [U.S. Census Bureau — Monthly Retail Trade Survey](https://www.census.gov/retail/index.html)
- **License:** Public Domain (U.S. Government Open Data)
- **Coverage:** Monthly sales data across retail and food service industries
- **Key Columns:** `month`, `year`, `naics_code`, `kind_of_business`, `industry`, `sales`

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Identify **top-performing industries** by total sales
- Find the **best and worst months** for retail and food sales
- Calculate **average sales** across industries and time periods
- Analyze **sales trends** over time
- Compare performance across **NAICS-classified business types**
